ABOUT US

A BRIEF INTRODUCTION

We started our operations in 2019 gaining the licence that very year meaning we are operating legally and well registered by the Ministry of Gender,Labour and Social Development.We are located in Ntinda opposite Weefunile driving school.We are here to provide all hardworking Ugandans with an opportunity to achieve their financial dreams and be successful.

OUR MAIN OBJECTIVE

Our main objective is to curb the high unemployment levels among the Ugandan youths by connecting them to well paying job opportunities in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East at large.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT FROM OTHER LABOUR EXPORTING COMPANIES

  • Our company is fraud free.
  • We do monthly follow ups on all those that go to work under our company
  • Our company doesnt conduct any human trafficking business
  • Our services are cheap
  • Our company is transparent and reliable
  • We protect our clients from any form of violence from the employers.
