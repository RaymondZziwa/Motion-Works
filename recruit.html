RECRUITMENT PROCESS

At Motion Works, critical information such as health status and background check of our candidates is a top priority and it has enabled us reach and keep the standards we are at right now. With this mentioned,all applicants shall under go a series of extensive screening so as to promote candidate information data integrity.One shall only be eligible to go and work after successfully completing all necessary steps and checks.

QUALIFICATIONS

Ugandan by nationality
Basic understanding of English
Age between 20-40 years
Valid National Id

LEVEL ONE

First screening session at Company head quarters

LEVEL TWO

MEDICAL TESTS AND DOCUMENT VALIDATION

The Company will conduct medical tests on all the applicants to get a clear picture of their health status.Further document screening shall also be done to get rid of forged documents.

LEVEL THREE

SELECTION

This is the third level where fit candidates will be selected depending on health status and their document validility.
Interviews at Company headquarters will be held to test various things such as language proficiency.

LEVEL FOUR

TRAINING

All successful candidates will attend a mandatory 1 week of training at company training facilities.

LEVEL FIVE

TRAVEL

Finally, those that would have successfully completed training shall be prepared to travel to thier respective places of work.

NOTE

FEES

Applicants shall be required to pay some fees.Currency is in shillings (UGX).
For more information call our client response team.
