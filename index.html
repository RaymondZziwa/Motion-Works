Welcome to Motion Works

Motion Works is a company well licensed in the Republic of Uganda with License No.E1907522. Motion Works aspires to be the leading labour exporting company in Uganda. Motion shall scout and recruit competent, skilled and reliable Ugandans to take up job opportunities abroad most especially in the Middle East.

What We have to Offer

Recruitment

Motion Works provides government regulated opportunities to willing, capable and qualified youths to work in various fields from overseas countries like Saudi Arabia and the MiddleEast at large.

Preparation

Motion Works trains all its recruited workers comprehensively and guides in comformity with the standards and requirements of the jobs available.

Travel

On completion of the Training and all medical checkups, all successfully recruited members are entitled to a free air ticket to their respective countries of work.
